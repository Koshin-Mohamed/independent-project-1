ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Koshin Mohamed, I'm 33 years old. I'm originally from Somalia but was raised and grew up in Toronto, Canada. I graduated from Ryerson University with a Bachelor in Commerce and from Seneca College with a Certificate in Global Logistics and Supply Chain Management for my post grad.

My work experience in mainly in the family business which is transport and wholesale of oil products. I have been and still am the Operations Manager.

I've always wanted to learn programming and have even tried on my own but quickly realized I needed more structure and help. This is why I decided to take a class at Moringa School. In addition, i'm quite entrepreneurial and have many ideas that I want to execute that willl require a website or app. For that reason, I want to be able to code and test it myslef to see if it is viable before investing in it. Lastly, this is the direction the world is going today, technology is touching almost every aspect of our lives and I don't want to get left behind. Plus it's fun to create things!

Some of my interests and hobbies include but are not limited to: playing and watching a variety of sports, working out, watching tv shows and movies, spending times with family, travelling, and building businesses.
